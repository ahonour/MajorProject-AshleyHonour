How to play Slice and Dice

Slice and Dice is a dice-based combat game. The goal is to defeat the enemy by rolling dice and using the results to attack. The game is played in turns. Each turn, you will roll your dice and use the final results to either attack the enemy, or help your units. The enemy will then attack you. The game continues until either you or the enemy is defeated.

Each turn, your units dice will be displayed in the center area, you may choose to click the dice to "lock" them, placing them in your unit area. Otherwise, click the "reroll" button to reroll every dice in the center area. You may reroll only a certain number of times per turn. Rolls do not carry over between turns, so keep rolling until you get the results you want!

Once every dice is locked, click on one of your unit's dice area to use them. You may only use each dice once per turn. After you have used all of them, click the "End Turn" button to end your turn. The enemy will then attack you, and then the next turn will begin.

After each fight, you will be offered a choice between three rewards. These rewards will modify values in the game, either making your characters stronger, or the enemies weaker. Also your units will revive and be fully healed after each fight.

The third fight will be a boss fight. The boss will be a signifigant challege, but if you can overcome it, you win the game!
